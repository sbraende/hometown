Top three acticities to do at Fornebu

Attend a festival or a concert

Fornebu hosts vibrant festivals and concerts, offering unforgettable experiences with top international and local artists.

Go for a walk in the local park

Fornebu's extensive park features serene walking trails, picturesque landscapes, and recreational activities for all ages.

Enjoy an icescream at the beach

Fornebu's stunning beaches provide perfect spots for relaxation, swimming, and enjoying beautiful coastal views.
